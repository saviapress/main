Obra - Little Red Riding Hood

Audio de las dos partes de la obra que le tocan a a Gabriela.

Parte 1



Yes, Little Red Riding Hood. Your grandmother is very sick. I want you to go to her house and take her this little basket with some bread and honey.




Parte 2



But don´t stop in the forest, and don´t talk to strangers. Do as I say, and get to your grandmother´s house.
