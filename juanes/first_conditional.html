Condicional 1

El first (1) conditional es para situaciones probables. Si la condicion (algo probable) se cumple, el resultado va muy seguramente a ocurrir tambi√©n.

Si nos apresuramos, alcanzaremos el bus

En first (1) conditional el verbo de la condicion va en presente y el verbo del resultado va en futuro (will + verbo) :

Ejercicio

Escribe las formas correctas de los verbos para formar condicional 1 conditional

 
  1. Give it to him if you ____________ him.
    1.   meet
    2.   will meet
  2. Unless she ____________ a rest, she'll have a headache.
    1.   has
    2.   doesn't have
  3. Even if I drive fast, we ____________ come on time, I'm afraid.
    1.   don't come
    2.   won't come
  4. What will you do if Bob ____________ ?
    1.   will refuse
    2.   refuses
  5. If we ____________ a car, we can visit a lot of places.
    1.   rent
    2.   will rent
  6. He'll join us on Monday as long as he ____________ a day off.
    1.   'll have
    2.   has
  7. If the weather is nice tomorrow, we ____________ plant the trees.
    1.   plant
    2.   'll plant
  8. You will get the job on condition that you _____________ hard.
    1.   work
    2.   will work
  9. The plane will take off on time provided that the runway _____________ free
    1.   is
    2.   will be
  10. As long as there's a suitable train, we _______________ there before dinner
    1.   are
    2.   'll be
